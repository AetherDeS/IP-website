About the project

Metaworld is a concept of a three-dimensional digital online world with virtual spaces and objects. The idea of developing a metaworld continues to evo lve, and is becoming more and more relevant. It is expected to go beyond video games and social networks. Remote working, decentralized management and digital identity are just some of the things that the meta-universe will support. It will also be multidimensional thanks to the connection of VR glasses and headsets, so users will be able to explore 3D spaces physically.
